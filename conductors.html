Conductors

   



Joseph Pollard White, Director
Originally from New York, Joseph Pollard White earned his Doctorate in Orchestral Conducting at the University of Washington, after studies at the Eastman School of Music and Indiana University. He has taught at Pacific Lutheran University, Northern Kentucky University and Christopher Newport University. He is active as a violist, composer and conductor.


Nathan Harrison, Associate Director
Originally from Anchorage, Alaska, Nathan Harrison studied at the University of Puget Sound, majoring in 'cello, and holds a Master's degree in Music Education from City University. He is the Director of Orchestras and Chamber Music at Kentwood High School in Kent, Washington.
