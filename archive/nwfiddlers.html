Chamber Music at Northwest Harvest

   
A chamber group of three violinists from the Ravenna String Orchestra provided a wonderful musical offering at the Northwest Harvest Cherry St. Food Bank.  The timing was fortuitous: three days before Thanksgiving and a drenching rain. Those in line for food backed up for blocks in record numbers. All at Northwest Harvest were thrilled and the players were delighted to be there. In fact they have expressed their desire to do a repeat performance.

If anyone has a chamber group and wants to offer an hour of music to those deeply appreciative, here’s a wonderful and unique place to play. Just send an e-mail to Judy Drake for details.  It will be quite a moving experience.
